Spanish Potato Omelette

Description

A tasty traditional spanish omelette recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs.
  • 2 medium sized potatos
  • A pinch of salt

    • Steps

    1. Whisk the eggs until the yolk and the white are mixed.
    2. Dice the potatoes and fry them.
    3. Cook the potatoes and the egg mix together.(tip:To flip the omelette use a plate and it will be easier to flip.)
